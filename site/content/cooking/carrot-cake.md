> Servings: 12 slices | Prepare Time: 10 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

> Serving: 199 Cals | 29.2g carbs | 6.7g fat | 8g protein

Half a kilo Carrot Cake with Chocolate Topping.

|Ingredients|
|---|---|
|Carrots|110g|
|Egg|2|
|Milk|40g|
|Oil|50g|
|Sugar|110g|
|Vanilla extract|12 drops|
|Flour|130g|
|Baking Powder|12g|

|Ingredients (Topping)|
|---|---|
|Butter|25g|
|50% Cocoa Powder|80g|
|Sugar|40g|
|Milk|250g|
|Instant Coffee|9g|
|Vanilla extract|12 drops|

Steps:
---
1. Peel and cut carrots, boil for 10 minutes
1. Blend eggs, orange juice, sugar, carrot and oil for 4 minutes
1. Mix it with flour and salt for 4 minutes
1. Add baking power and mix by hand for 30 seconds
1. pour in a margarine greased baking tray and bake for **30 minutes at 160 C**

Steps (Topping):
---
1. Mix all and cook on low heat for 10 minutes or until desired consistency.
